👩‍💻 Meet Anjali Yadav

Your AI & Coding Mentor

Hello! I'm Anjali, the creator of CodeWithAnjalii — a platform dedicated to making cutting-edge technology accessible, understandable, and exciting for everyone!

From Generative AI and Machine Learning to Full-Stack Development and Automation, I'm here to guide you through your tech journey with clarity, passion, and real-world applications.

🎯 My Mission

To bridge the gap between complex technology and practical application. I believe that anyone can learn to code, master AI tools, and build amazing things — regardless of their background or previous experience.

My goal is to create a learning environment where students don't just memorize code, but truly understand the 'why' behind every concept, building confidence and problem-solving skills that last a lifetime.

💡 My Teaching Philosophy

I believe in learning by doing. Every lesson is designed with three core principles:

  • Clarity: Complex concepts broken down into digestible steps
  • Practice: Hands-on projects and real-world applications
  • Purpose: Understanding not just 'how' but 'why' and 'when' to use each concept

I teach in both Hindi and English to ensure every Indian student can learn comfortably in their preferred language.
